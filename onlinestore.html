KashmirProducts.com

Kashmirproducts.com, started as an online destination exclusively for flower bulbs and plants of Kashmir through its sister concern Royal Treasure Flowers Kashmir, back in 2008.The encouraging response by our valuable customers and enquiries about genuine Kashmiri products from all over the world ultimately landed up with our venture www.kashmirproducts.com. Kashmirproducts.com is Kashmir based online webshop of 100% guaranteed genuine Kashmir products viz. Kashmir Arts and Crafts, Saffron and Honey, Plants and Flowers and many other exclusive Kashmir Brand Products.

Kashmir has a history of rich heritage of Arts, Crafts, skills, knowledge and traditional foods acknowledged all over world. Kashmir represents a manufacturing class of Kashmiri Handicrafts. Kashmiri handicrafts are known throughout the world for their artistic work and grandeur. The Kashmir saffron, Kashmir shawls , Kashmir carpets and Kashmir paper machie works have been recognized globally for their various natural looks and tastes.

At Kashmirproducts.com we bring the Kashmiri products directly from the source to the customer, thereby discouraging the middleman who exploits both the manufacturers and customers.

Our sole motto is to work sincerely and put dedicated efforts to satisfy a customer by supplying the products what actually he/she wants from Kashmir . We firmly believe that our success is largely due to our passion for providing our customers a memorable online shopping experience of Genuine Kashmiri Products. Our dedicated team works round the clock to personally make sure packages reach you on time and in good condition. Our robust customer care team is always available not only to our valuable customers but to anyone from any part of the world to reply any queries regarding various Kashmir Brand Products.
