Royal Treasure Flowers Kashmir

Royal Treasure Flowers Kashmir is a Flower and Plants Nursery located in Kashmir Valley, where its humus soil and perfect climate bring about the best possible growing conditions. Kashmir Valley is famous the world over for the enchanting beauty, the nature has liberally bestowed on it. Abundantly rich in natural resources like lush green forests, beautiful and large lakes, snow capped mountain ranges, heavily water laden rivers, cold desert, alpine pastures, rare and varied flora and fauna, and what not.

We have been in the seed and nursery business for over 15 years and have standardized techniques for raising and propagation of flower bulbs and seeds successfully. The major field of interest include cut Flower production, Bulb production in Tulips, Lilium, Hyacinths, Carnation, Daffodils and Plants including Orchard Plants. Our purpose during these years has been to bring people closer to nature through gardening.

The vast majority of Bulbs & Plants we offer are grown in our own fields and greenhouses to ensure that our products meet the standards of quality and permit us to guarantee delivery at the time that is best for the plants and our customers.

We employ packing methods that are superb, plants & bulbs seldom have trouble in transit. The materials we use include cardboard, recycled newsprint, plastic bags, combined in ways that are ingenious and effective. Every shipment includes a detailed list of its contents.

Our location allows us to deliver at the right time for planting throughout the country by matching the progress of the season, and orders are often delivered in more than one shipment according to the requirements of the plants.

Our Customer Service Department is headed by Mr. Nazir Ahmed Dar qualified Masters in the field of Botany and more than 15 years of experience in floriculture and staffed mostly by gardeners so it's unusual to receive a question that be cannot be handled. If you cannot find the answer to your gardening questions on our site, you may call for advice at +91 9469100020 between 9 a.m. and 9 p.m. weekdays.

We have Online Store here at KashmirProducts.com that features many of the plants and flower bulbs we offer. Besides our online store we have a retail store at Kursu Raj Bagh, Srinagar, Kashmir and display garden that feature many of the plants we offer. Please note that varieties, sizes and prices of the plants at the Store may vary from those in the catalogue or on our Web site.

The Nursery is registered with Jammu and Kashmir Government Department of Floriculture for growing and selling of Floricultural products under prescribed rules and regulations.
